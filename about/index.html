My Journey

How Cloudy to Clear
came to be.

After more than 20 years in the Biotech and Pharmaceutical industry, I experienced firsthand how demanding professional life can be — and how challenging it is to stay organized when you are constantly juggling work, family responsibilities, and everything in between.

I watched colleagues, friends, and even myself struggle with cluttered home offices, overwhelming closets, and kitchen drawers that became a source of daily frustration rather than ease. The stress of a disorganized space quietly compounds every other pressure in a busy life.

“I took the skills I developed in my corporate career — attention to detail, project management, and problem-solving — and combined them with my natural love for creating order.”

That journey inspired me to start Cloudy to Clear. Now, I get to help other busy professionals and families in the San Francisco Bay Area simplify their lives, one organized space at a time.

Organizing spaces isn’t just a job for me — it’s a true passion. I believe in the transformative power of decluttering and organizing, and I’m here to help you experience the joy, clarity, and calm that comes from living in a space that truly supports your life.
